While in design roles, I realized many teams are so focused on improving the customer experience that they miss opportunities to improve their team experience. The presentations below were created for different companies with the objective of increasing and improving internal communication in order to foster a better work environment and better development output.

Requirements Writing for Designers

Requirements writing skills are useful not only for Product Owners, but also for Designers. Delivering visual and functional documentation for designs increases clarity for less visual coworkers and reduces assumptions during development.

Cross-functional Collaboration

Working cross-functionally is key to the success of any product. When departments are siloed, teams should develop a set of guardrails, strategies, and agreed upon processes to improve communication and work towards collaboration.
