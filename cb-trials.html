Trial Products

Work Type:Product Management, Research
Date: 9.2019 - Current
Company:Comcast Business

Comcast Business regularly runs internal and partnership product trials. Products can range anywhere from a custom-built application to a third-party product that compliments other services. The trials have various objectives, but each involves customer outreach, process creation, surveys and interviews, and data analysis. Below are a few examples of trials where I've created the process, surveys, and final readouts.

Nomorobo Call Blocking

Xfinity offers a very popular spam and robo call blocking service for residential customers. Comcast Business set up a similar partnership and opened the service up to business customers for no charge.

Objectives:

  • Determine if call blocking has business demand
  • Gather results on customer satisfaction

Zipwhip Messaging

Sales engineers regularly get asked if text messaging is available in the Comcast Business Unified Communications product. To determine if messaging is a good long-term investment, the team partnered with Zipwhip to set up a 90-day trial.

Objectives:

  • Determine if small business customers will utilize messaging
  • Gather information on willingness to pay for the service

WiFi Pods

Xfinity does well selling wifi extenders to residential customers. Comcast Business partnered with a company with more powerful extenders for larger locations. Customers were set up with pods, at no initial cost, to test the product in various types of business locations.

Objectives:

  • Determine wifi pods have business demand
  • Learn about the quality of the product
  • Gather information on willingness to pay for the service
